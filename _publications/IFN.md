<h3>üìù Abstract</h3>
<p class="abstract-text">We propose the Intelligent Fusion Network (IFN), a novel architecture combining dual attention, feature refinement, and multiplicative fusion to enhance speech emotion recognition (SER) performance and reproducibility.
